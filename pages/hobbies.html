My Hobbies & Interests

Life beyond code: what keeps me inspired and balanced

Life Beyond Code

While I'm passionate about software development, I believe in maintaining a well-rounded life. My hobbies help me stay creative, healthy, and inspired - qualities that enhance my problem-solving abilities as a developer.

Hiking

Exploring nature's beauty while challenging myself physically. Vancouver offers incredible hiking opportunities that I regularly take advantage of.

Favorite Trails Completed:

  • Grouse Mountain - The Grouse Grind
  • Lynn Canyon - Rice Lake Loop
  • Stawamus Chief Mountain - First Peak
  • Sea to Sky Gondola - Sea to Summit Trail
  • Garibaldi Lake - Panorama Ridge

Reading

Books expand my perspective and help me unwind. I enjoy a mix of non-fiction for personal growth and fiction for creative inspiration.

Recently Read Books:

  • Atomic Habits by James Clear
  • The Charisma Myth by Olivia Fox Cabane
  • Designing Data-Intensive Applications by Martin Kleppmann
  • Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir
  • The Psychology of Money by Morgan Housel

Reading Goals:

I aim to read at least one technical book and one non-technical book each month to continue expanding my knowledge.

Singing

Singing is my creative outlet and stress reliever. I find it meditative and energizing at the same time.

Musical Interests:

  • Participate in local karaoke events
  • Practice vocal techniques during my free time
  • Enjoy a wide range of musical genres from pop to classical
  • Occasionally record covers of my favorite songs

Music provides a perfect balance to the logical thinking required in programming, helping me maintain creative thinking skills.
